Priority areas — WA

High-need communities first.

Western Australia's North West has been devastated by Cyclone Narelle. Exmouth, Carnarvon and Shark Bay are in active recovery. Meanwhile, fuel costs are hitting regional and remote WA communities hard — from the Kimberley to the Pilbara to the Gascoyne.
