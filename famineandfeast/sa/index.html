PRIORITY AREAS — SA

Drought, heat, and distance.

South Australia faces a triple pressure: prolonged drought in the state's north and west, extreme heatwaves that strain households, and remote communities cut off by distance. Port Augusta and Coober Pedy are food distribution hubs for vast inland regions. Tourists on the Nullarbor and Oodnadatta Track are often isolated with little warning.

TOURISTS & TRAVELLERS

Not just for locals.

SA's Nullarbor Plain, Flinders Ranges, and Outback tracks draw hundreds of thousands of travellers yearly. When supplies or fuel run short, PantryShare AU connects tourists with the nearest communities who can help.
