PRIORITY AREAS — NSW

Floods, fire, and rising costs.

NSW has been hit repeatedly by flood disasters along the Hawkesbury, Clarence, and Lismore river systems, as well as devastating bushfires in the highlands and south coast. Rural and regional communities face ongoing recovery while navigating rising fuel and food costs. Travellers through the Blue Mountains, Snowy Mountains, and coastal holiday towns also rely on local community networks when conditions change.

TOURISTS & TRAVELLERS

Not just for locals.

NSW receives over 30 million visitors annually. The Blue Mountains, Snowy Mountains, Hunter Valley, and Byron Bay attract tourists who can be stranded by floods, bushfires, or road closures. PantryShare AU provides a direct line to local community support.
