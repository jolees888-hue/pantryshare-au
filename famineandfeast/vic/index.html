PRIORITY AREAS — VIC

Recovery takes years, not weeks.

Victoria's communities have faced back-to-back crises — Black Summer bushfires in East Gippsland, flooding across north-east Victoria, and persistent drought in the Mallee. Meanwhile, Melbourne's outer suburbs face some of Australia's highest cost-of-living pressure. Tourists in the alpine resorts and Great Ocean Road can be stranded by fire closures and flood events.

TOURISTS & TRAVELLERS

Not just for locals.

Victoria's Great Ocean Road, Alpine National Park, and Yarra Valley draw millions of visitors. Bushfire closures and alpine blizzards regularly strand tourists — PantryShare AU connects them with local community support networks.
