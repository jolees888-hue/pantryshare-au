PRIORITY AREAS — ACT

A capital city with real need.

Despite being Australia's most educated and highest-income territory on average, the ACT has significant pockets of disadvantage — Tuggeranong and Belconnen face real food insecurity. The 2020 Namadgi bushfires destroyed habitat and impacted surrounding communities. Tourism to Canberra, Tidbinbilla, and the Snowy Mountains passes through the ACT, making it a key hub for community food networks.

TOURISTS & TRAVELLERS

Not just for locals.

Canberra receives over 4 million visitors annually for institutions, events, and Snowy Mountains transit. PantryShare AU supports travellers who need local food connections — and residents navigating rising costs in a city where the cost of living often contradicts its reputation.
