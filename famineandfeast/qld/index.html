Priority areas — QLD

High-need communities first.

North Queensland is one of Australia's most disaster-prone regions — cyclones, flooding, drought and extreme heat affect communities from Cairns to Townsville to Mackay every year. Tourists, grey nomads and travellers also get stranded when roads close and shelves empty. PantryShare AU builds the local food networks that carry communities through.

Not just for locals — travellers welcome too.

Travelling through North Queensland? Cyclones and floods can close roads for days. PantryShare AU is for everyone passing through — not just locals. If you're stuck and need supplies, or you have extra to share, post it.
